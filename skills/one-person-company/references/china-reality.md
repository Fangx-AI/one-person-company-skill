# China Reality

Use this reference when a product will be sold, hosted, distributed, or paid for in the mainland China context.

默认判断语境是中国大陆。海外模式不能直接照搬；海外案例只能说明某种商业机制可能成立，不能自动证明它适配国内。回答必须重新检查渠道、支付、合规、信任、交付五个条件。

## Common Execution Constraints

- **ICP filing**: Mainland hosting normally requires备案. It can slow launch, fail for unsuitable content/business scope, or create future takedown risk. Many small founders start with Hong Kong or overseas hosting for speed, then file later only when the product proves revenue.
- **Payments**: WeChat Pay and Alipay improve conversion but require entity setup and compliance work. For validation, manual transfer, 小报童/知识星球/有赞/小鹅通/飞书群/微信群 can be faster.
- **Distribution**: WeChat public accounts, Zhihu, Jike, Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Bilibili, podcasts, and private communities each reward different content forms. Do not prescribe "do content" without selecting the channel and first content angle.
- **Platform dependency**: Xiaohongshu/Douyin/WeChat traffic can change quickly. Treat platform reach as rented distribution, not defensibility.
- **Enterprise trust**: Domestic B2B buyers may need invoices, contracts, local support, data handling clarity, and relationship trust. A solo founder should start where these requirements are light.
- **AI product risk**: Generic AI output is easy to copy. Charge for workflow completion, domain-specific data, saved labor, compliance confidence, or distribution advantage.

## China-Specific Judgment Questions

Ask:

1. Can this be sold through a channel the founder already understands?
2. Does the buyer trust a one-person vendor for this job?
3. Is a personal account enough for validation, or is company/entity setup needed before payment?
4. Does hosting, filing, data, or content review risk block launch?
5. Is the user buying a product, a service, a template, a group, a result, or reduced uncertainty?

## Practical Recommendation Pattern

Prefer phased advice:

- **Validation phase**: fastest legal/low-risk way to test payment.
- **Revenue phase**: add payment, delivery, refund, support, and case proof.
- **Scale phase**: only after repeat sales, consider ICP filing, domestic hosting, automation, company entity, and formal channel partnerships.

Never default to "go file ICP first" unless mainland hosting is essential to the first paid test.
