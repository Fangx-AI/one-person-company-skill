# Knowledge Products

date_checked: 2026-05-14

Purpose: Judge manuals, courses, playbooks, and paid reports by buyer promise and trust path.

## direct competitors

- 小报童 paid columns.
- 知识星球 paid community and archive.
- Gumroad digital downloads.
- Substack paid publication, Teachable/Coursera-style courses.

## adjacent substitutes

- Free newsletters, blog posts, podcasts, GitHub awesome lists, YouTube/Bilibili tutorials.
- 微信群 and community Q&A.

## free substitutes

- Public articles, free PDFs, open-source handbooks, AI-generated summaries.

## high-price alternatives

- Consulting, coaching, cohort course, company training, done-for-you implementation.

## payment mechanism

- One-time guide/report purchase.
- Paid column subscription.
- Community annual fee.
- Cohort tuition.
- Consulting or implementation upsell.

## evidence boundary

- Knowledge products sell when they reduce uncertainty, save time, or help decisions.
- Page count and generic expertise do not prove willingness to pay.

## one-person company wedge

- Sell a specific promise such as 20-case teardown, checklist, pricing map, or 7-day action plan.
- Start with 10 buyers at 49/99/199 RMB.

## stop-loss

- 10 target users ask for free preview but nobody pays.
- Refund/complaint says the promise is too generic.
- Buyers cannot name the decision it helps.
