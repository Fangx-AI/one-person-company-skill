# Templates And Boilerplates

date_checked: 2026-05-14

Purpose: Judge templates, boilerplates, and reusable assets by saved time and buyer urgency.

## direct competitors

- Tailwind UI: paid UI components/templates.
- ShipFast: SaaS boilerplate and launch kit.
- Notion templates marketplaces.
- Framer/Webflow templates and code starter kits.

## adjacent substitutes

- Open-source starters, GitHub repos, AI-generated code, tutorials, design systems.

## free substitutes

- Free templates, starter repos, copied landing pages, component libraries.

## high-price alternatives

- Custom design/development agency, consultant setup, private template customization.

## payment mechanism

- One-time template buyout.
- Bundle sale.
- License tiers.
- Updates/community access.
- Customization service upsell.

## evidence boundary

- Template markets prove buyers pay to save time and reduce uncertainty.
- A template being useful does not prove it is differentiated or trusted enough to buy.

## one-person company wedge

- Pick one buyer and outcome: SaaS launch kit, creator media kit, local merchant mini-site, AI tool landing page.
- Pre-sell 20 copies at 49/99/199 RMB before polishing.

## stop-loss

- People ask for inspiration but do not buy.
- Buyers need heavy customization after purchase.
- Free alternatives satisfy the same job.
