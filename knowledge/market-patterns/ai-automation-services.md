# AI Automation Services

date_checked: 2026-05-14

Purpose: Judge AI automation ideas by productized service boundary and repeatability.

## direct competitors

- Zapier: no-code automation platform.
- Make: visual automation workflow builder.
- n8n: workflow automation and self-hosted automation.
- AI automation agencies and RPA service providers.

## adjacent substitutes

- Manual assistants, operations staff, Excel macros, scripts, SaaS integrations.
- Enterprise RPA tools.

## free substitutes

- Open-source n8n/self-hosted workflows, free Zapier tiers, scripts, ChatGPT manual SOPs.

## high-price alternatives

- Enterprise RPA implementation, custom software outsourcing, system integrators, consultants.

## payment mechanism

- Fixed-scope implementation fee.
- Monthly retainer/maintenance.
- Workflow package.
- Usage-based automation platform subscription.
- Training and documentation fee.

## evidence boundary

- Automation demand exists when manual workflow cost is visible.
- A company saying automation sounds useful does not prove repeatable delivery.

## one-person company wedge

- Sell one fixed workflow with fixed input/output, e.g. lead intake to CRM to follow-up draft.
- Price 999/1999/4999 RMB with one revision boundary.

## stop-loss

- First 3 orders all require custom systems and unclear input/output.
- Delivery time exceeds price-supported margin.
- Client needs procurement/security before small trial.
