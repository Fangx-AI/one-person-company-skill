# Local Lead Generation

date_checked: 2026-05-14

Purpose: Judge tools and services that help local merchants acquire leads or inquiries.

## direct competitors

- 美团 and 大众点评 merchant traffic products.
- 企业微信 lead capture and private-domain operation.
- Local SEO/review-management tools.
- Xiaohongshu/Douyin local merchant operation services.

## adjacent substitutes

- Agency代运营, flyer/community promotion, referral groups, group-buying campaigns.
- Manual CRM and WeChat follow-up.

## free substitutes

- Posting on social platforms manually, free map listings, community posts, friend referrals.

## high-price alternatives

- Full local marketing agency, paid ads, KOL placement, CRM setup, sales outsourcing.

## payment mechanism

- Monthly service fee.
- Lead package.
- Performance commission.
- Setup fee for Enterprise WeChat/CRM.
- Content operation retainer.

## evidence boundary

- Local merchants pay when leads convert to appointments/orders.
- Views, likes, and followers do not prove lead quality or payback.

## one-person company wedge

- Pick one vertical such as dental, beauty, fitness, home repair, education, or local restaurants.
- Sell 7-day lead experiment for 299/999 RMB with explicit inquiry count.

## stop-loss

- Merchant cannot define lead value.
- 10 merchants will not pay for a small test.
- Generated leads are low intent or impossible to attribute.
