# Case Intelligence

date_checked: 2026-05-14

Purpose: Judge case libraries, databases, trend reports, and decision intelligence products.

## direct competitors

- Trends.vc: paid trend and business research.
- Exploding Topics: trend discovery and paid research workflow.
- FounderPal: founder-focused AI/business tooling.
- Starter Story / Indie Hackers-style founder story databases.

## adjacent substitutes

- Free case articles, podcasts, GitHub lists, newsletters, VC reports, community posts.

## free substitutes

- Google search, Perplexity/AI summaries, public directories, Reddit/HN threads.

## high-price alternatives

- Market research firms, consulting reports, expert networks, private lead databases.

## payment mechanism

- Subscription for searchable database.
- Paid reports and trend briefings.
- Seat-based research access.
- Custom research or lead-list service.

## evidence boundary

- People pay when cases are normalized into decisions, not when stories are merely collected.
- A large archive does not prove value unless it changes product, pricing, or channel decisions.

## one-person company wedge

- Pick one decision: product idea validation, competitor pricing, channel choice, or first-customer path.
- Sell 10-case decision report for 99/299 RMB.

## stop-loss

- Users browse but do not use it for a decision.
- No one pays for a sample report.
- Case freshness becomes too costly without a paid output.
